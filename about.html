//01 About · founded 2026

small team. big problems.

exempli is a small bench of senior engineers who care about software that fits. We build custom — not because it's fashionable, but because most teams have outgrown the off-the-shelf tools they started with.

//02 Mission

We exist to help teams replace the half-dozen apps that almost work with one thing that fits — designed around how they actually run, maintained by the people who built it.

//03 What we believe
001 · Bias to fit

Software designed around your team, not a generic best practice.

Off-the-shelf is optimized for the average customer. You're not the average. The fit matters more than the feature list.

002 · Less stack

Fewer tools, tighter integration, lower mental load.

Every app you adopt is a tax on attention. We build the one thing that quietly replaces three of them.

003 · Stay on

We maintain what we ship. No throwaway demos.

Production-grade from day one. We're around when reality hits the model, not in a deck six weeks ago.

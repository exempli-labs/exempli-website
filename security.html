//01 Security & trust

your data. your code. your software.

Custom software means letting someone inside your business. Here's exactly how we treat that access — what we take, what we don't, and what you keep. No legalese, no fine print.

//02 The commitments
001 · Data & privacy

We see the minimum. You keep everything.

An audit needs visibility, not custody. We work against the smallest slice of data that answers the question — anonymized or synthetic where possible.

  • Least-access by default: scoped accounts, revocable the day we're done
  • GDPR-aligned handling; EU data stays in the EU when you need it to
  • Your data is never used to train anything, sold, or shared
  • Full deletion of working copies on request — verified, not promised
002 · IP & ownership

You own the code. All of it.

The software we build for you is yours — source, infrastructure config, documentation, the lot. We don't rent you your own product.

  • Full IP assignment on delivery, in plain contract language
  • Standard, boring stacks — any competent team can take over
  • Code escrow available for the cautious
  • No lock-in: leaving us costs you a handshake, not a migration
003 · Confidentiality

NDA first. Names never.

Most engagements start with an NDA before we see anything — ours or yours, whichever's stricter. What we learn inside your business stays there.

  • NDA by default before any audit begins
  • Named access list — you know exactly who can see what
  • No case studies, logos, or numbers published without written consent
004 · Secure development

Boring, disciplined, auditable.

Security isn't a feature we add at the end. It's how the work happens, from the first commit.

  • Secrets live in managed vaults — never in code, chat, or email
  • Least-privilege access for every environment, reviewed monthly
  • Dependencies audited and pinned; updates are deliberate, not drive-by
  • Every change reviewed before merge; every release versioned and reversible
005 · Hosting & compliance

Compliance-ready from day one.

We deploy on infrastructure your auditors already trust, configured so a future SOC 2 or ISO journey starts from "mostly done" rather than zero.

  • SOC 2-ready cloud providers; EU or US data residency, your choice
  • Encryption in transit and at rest, everywhere, no exceptions
  • Automated backups with actually-tested recovery
  • Audit logs your compliance team can read without calling us
006 · What we don't do

The quiet part, out loud.

Some of the industry's defaults aren't ours. For the avoidance of any doubt:

  • No analytics, trackers, or phone-home code hidden in your product
  • No reselling, benchmarking, or "anonymized insights" from your data
  • No shadow subcontracting — the people you meet do the work
  • No hostage tactics: access, docs, and keys are handed over as we go
